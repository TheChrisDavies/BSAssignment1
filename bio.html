Biography

Hi, my name is Chris Davies and I am a full stack developer located in Toronto, Ontario. I have a wide variety of experience, from government health care enterprise web applications to smaller sites for friends and events. My experience includes web applications using C#, ASP.NET, HTML, JavaScript and CSS. I got into this line of work because I wanted to help people and feel that I can achieve this through my development work.

I live in Toronto and volunteer at a lot of places outside of my development work. I work hard to try to find a balance between work and personal development. When I am not working, I find time to read, run, play video games and develop personal projects
